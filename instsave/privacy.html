Privacy Policy

Data collected

In short, the app itself does not collect any data from you. Yes, we don't use any data tracking technology for growth purposes, or even to count how many users we have. We just want to provide a secure text processing environment and don't care about the size of our users or how our application is used by them.

Other than that, please have confidence in our service, all private information remains on your device forever and we have no control over it.

Cookies

Cookies are files with a small amount of data, usually used as anonymous unique identifiers. This information is sent from the website you visit to your browser and stored on the device.

InstSave does not explicitly use cookie technology. However, third party code or web pages that we use may collect cookies to improve their services, and you can choose whether to accept or reject these cookies and know when they are sent to your device.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. Therefore, we recommend that you check this page periodically for updates. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately upon posting on this page.
